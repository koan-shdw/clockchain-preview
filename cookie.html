Cookie Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how D4D Sàrl ("D4D," "Clockchain," "we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on the Clockchain website ("Website"). By using the Website, you acknowledge that cookies may be used as described in this Cookie Policy. Where required by applicable law, we will request your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device.

You can review or change your choice at any time using Cookie settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, tablet, mobile phone, or other device when you visit a website. Cookies allow a website to recognize your browser, remember preferences, improve functionality, analyze website performance, and enhance the overall user experience. In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies such as local storage, web beacons, pixels, or software development kits (SDKs), where applicable.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation and security of the Website. They enable functions such as website navigation, session management, security protections, load balancing, form submission, and authentication (if user accounts become available). These cookies cannot be disabled through our cookie preference tools because the Website cannot function properly without them.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember choices you make, such as language preferences, regional settings, accessibility preferences, and previously selected options. These cookies improve your experience but are not strictly required for the Website to operate.

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the Website. They may collect information such as pages visited, time spent on pages, navigation paths, browser type, device type, operating system, general geographic region, and referring websites. This information is used to improve Website performance and user experience. Where required by law, analytics cookies are only activated after obtaining your consent.

Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us measure and improve Website speed, reliability, and availability. They assist in identifying technical issues and optimizing overall performance.

Marketing Cookies

If we engage in future advertising or marketing campaigns, marketing cookies may be used to measure campaign effectiveness, remember marketing preferences, limit repetitive advertisements, and personalize promotional content. Clockchain does not currently use advertising cookies for behavioral advertising unless specifically disclosed.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by trusted third-party service providers. Depending on the services we use, these providers may include website analytics providers, cloud hosting providers, content delivery networks (CDNs), customer support platforms, embedded video providers, and security and fraud detection services. These providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. How Long Cookies Remain

Cookies may be either session cookies, which expire automatically when you close your browser, or persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a specified period or until you delete them. Retention periods vary depending on the purpose of each cookie.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view stored cookies, delete cookies, block all cookies, block cookies from specific websites, and receive notifications before cookies are stored. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect Website functionality.

6. Cookie Consent

Where required by applicable law, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy rules applicable in certain jurisdictions, we will request your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. You may withdraw or modify your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie preference tools, where available.

7. Do Not Track

Some web browsers offer a "Do Not Track" setting. Because there is currently no universally accepted standard for responding to Do Not Track signals, the Website may not respond to such requests.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with a revised Effective Date. Your continued use of the Website after the updated Cookie Policy becomes effective constitutes acknowledgment of the revised policy.
