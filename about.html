About

The company behind the clock.

D4D Sàrl is a Swiss company building Clockchain: a decentralized, cryptographically verifiable global time standard, anchored on-chain every second.

What we do

Time underpins every financial transaction, legal agreement, and autonomous decision, yet the clocks the internet runs on cannot be independently verified. Clockchain replaces trust in a time source with proof: a physical network of atomic clocks, national time institutes, and satellite systems, reconciled by AI and validated on-chain through Proof-of-Time consensus.

The network produces a signed, permanent timestamp every second, and exposes it through three services: logging and authentication, smart contract scheduling, and a Timestamp API.
