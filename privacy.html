Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

D4D Sàrl ("D4D," "Clockchain," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide when visiting the Clockchain website or using our services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we may disclose it, and the choices available to you.

1. Who We Are

Clockchain is a blockchain-based global time synchronization and timestamping network developed and operated by D4D Sàrl. For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:
D4D Sàrl
Email: legal@d4d.group
Website: https://clockchain.network

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

This may include your name, email address, company name, job title, telephone number, information submitted through contact forms, and information provided when requesting demonstrations, documentation, newsletters, or support. You may choose not to provide this information, although certain services may not be available.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect technical information including IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, pages visited, referring website, date and time of visits, session duration, cookie identifiers, and general geographic region.

Blockchain Data

Clockchain provides cryptographically verifiable timestamps and blockchain-based services. Data written to the Clockchain Network may become permanently recorded on a distributed ledger. Because blockchain records are designed to be immutable, they generally cannot be modified or deleted after confirmation. Users should avoid submitting personal information directly to blockchain transactions unless specifically required by the application they are using.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to operate and maintain our website, respond to inquiries, provide requested information, improve our products and services, analyze website performance, detect fraud or unauthorized activity, maintain security, comply with applicable laws, communicate product updates, and send newsletters (only when requested).

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, improve website functionality, measure website traffic, understand visitor behavior, and improve user experience. Where required by law, we will request your consent before placing non-essential cookies. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, although portions of the website may not function properly. See our Cookie Policy for details.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers to operate portions of our website and services, including website hosting providers, cloud infrastructure providers, analytics providers, email delivery providers, customer support platforms, and content delivery networks. These providers receive only the information necessary to perform their services on our behalf.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information to trusted service providers, when required by law, to comply with legal obligations, to protect our rights, to prevent fraud or security incidents, and during a merger, acquisition, or business restructuring.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide requested services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Information permanently recorded on the Clockchain Network cannot generally be altered or deleted because of the decentralized nature of blockchain technology.

8. Security

We employ commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Because our users may be located worldwide, personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including to access your personal information, correct inaccurate information, request deletion, restrict processing, object to processing, withdraw consent, receive a copy of your information, and lodge a complaint with the appropriate supervisory authority. Requests may be submitted to legal@d4d.group.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business and professional users and are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date. Continued use of our website after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

14. Contact Us
