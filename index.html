What is Clockchain

A verifiable global time standard: time you can prove.

A decentralized network of trusted time sources, from certified atomic clocks to national time institutes and satellite navigation systems, aggregated by AI and validated on-chain every second. Every timestamp is cryptographically signed, permanent, and independently verifiable by anyone.

Clockchain Services

Three services. One clock.

A complete toolkit for any product that depends on the integrity of a moment. Easy to integrate, instantly verifiable, decentralized by default.

01 Logging, Authentication & Timestamps

Timestamp and log data every second onto Clockchain in a simple, decentralized, cost-effective way. Instantly authenticate from any device, anywhere.

  • Industry-standard hashing for any asset
  • Advanced timestamp verification
  • Search and audit your full log history
02 Smart Contract Scheduling & Execution

Schedule and execute critical services with the first verifiable blockchain time, a scheduling and execution system you can trust. Never miss a deadline again.

  • Time triggers with cryptographic proof of execution
  • Webhook notifications
  • Full deployment monitoring
03 Timestamp API

Access, verify, and stamp anything, every second, on any device. Clockchain-verified timestamps for UTC, GPS, TAI, ISO 8601, and RFC 3339, with microsecond precision.

  • Unlimited API requests with full API access
  • Webhook notifications and bulk operations
  • SLA guarantee
How it works

Three primitives. One clock.

A decentralized physical network of trusted time sources, aggregated by AI, validated on-chain through Proof-of-Time consensus.

01

Decentralized timestamping via DePIN.

A physical network of trusted time sources, from atomic clocks to national time institutes and satellite systems, ensures tamper-proof timestamping across every region.

DePIN · Distributed sources
02

AI + blockchain integration.

AI models reconcile time data across heterogeneous sources in real time, then propagate the validated state to nodes worldwide for instant consensus.

Adaptive consensus · Sub-second
03

Proof-of-Time consensus.

A new consensus mechanism that aggregates global time data and validates it on-chain: accuracy and consistency across every block, every second.

PoT · On-chain validation
